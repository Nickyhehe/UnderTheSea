About Us

The UnderTheSea website was founded by Nicky Dharmawan in 5/27/2022. This website was established to provide education about the vast underwater life.

UnderTheSea aims to provide educational materials for people of all ages and backgrounds from preschoolers to senior citizens.

With a variety of knowledge and information about marine animals, we hope that people who bring this website get knowledge and awareness of the importance of caring for the ocean
